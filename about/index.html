About

Leave your mark in my projects

On this website you can leave your mark in games I’m currently making and give feedback on my completed projects, propose improvements, ideas and give your opinion about game design

During development processes, I will regularly ask you via polls your preferences between several options or let you express yourself about. If you don’t want to participate: no problem. I understand people who simply want to see the progress of projects they are interested in and update logs.

Who am I ?

I’m Ensikyo, an independent french developer. I like making apps and games. I like programming for the creative abilities it grants. I’m not good at drawing, that’s why my first games will have simple graphics but I’m practicing and I’m making progress. Feel free to ask any question you have.
